## Design Context

### Users
Independent developers and hackers who use AI coding tools daily and want a calmer, more durable working environment. They are usually juggling active branches, terminal-heavy workflows, and ongoing conversations with AI assistants, and they want to keep momentum without reconstructing project context every time they switch tasks.

### Brand Personality
Sharp, ambitious, calm-under-pressure. 2code should feel like a serious tool for focused builders: confident without hype, technical without looking cold, and expressive without drifting into flashy AI clichés.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial futurism with warm architectural tactility. The site should feel like a contemporary product editorial rather than a template SaaS page: generous whitespace, asymmetry, distinctive serif-led typography, and a light-first palette derived from the ribbed wood hero image. Visual references should suggest crafted structure, flow lines, and composed systems, not neon sci-fi.

### Design Principles
Preserve flow by keeping the interface legible, calm, and structurally clear.
Show system depth through composition and narrative, not generic feature-card repetition.
Keep the human collaborator centered even when presenting AI capabilities.
Use warm, tactile visual language to differentiate 2code from common AI-startup aesthetics.
Treat the supplied hero image as a defining brand asset that informs the whole page.
